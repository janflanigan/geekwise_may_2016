Here's a Little Bit About Me

I am a hobbyist photographer, and primitive mixed media artist. I combine my original drawings, actual dead flies, and photography to make pieces of work that sell for tens of dollars.

After spending the last 20 years in a state prison (as an employee), I was ready to indulge in a more rewarding activity. Taking photos of flies on cartoon drawings was the clear choice.

A piece of secret nerd information that I would die of humiliation if anyone were to find out: I had a crush on Mr. Spock.
